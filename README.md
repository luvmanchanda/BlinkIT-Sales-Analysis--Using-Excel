# Blinkit Sales Analysis using Excel

## Overview

This project presents an interactive **Microsoft Excel dashboard** designed to analyze Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, KPI analysis, and interactive visualizations, enabling efficient business performance monitoring.

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance across different product categories and outlets.
* Evaluate customer satisfaction using product ratings.
* Identify sales trends and inventory distribution patterns.
* Support data-driven business decisions through interactive reporting.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Sales** – Overall revenue generated.
* **Average Sales** – Average revenue per sale.
* **Number of Items Sold** – Total products sold.
* **Average Rating** – Overall customer satisfaction score.

## Dashboard Insights

* Total Sales by Item Type
* Total Sales by Fat Content
* Fat Content Analysis by Outlet
* Sales by Outlet Size, Location, and Type
* Sales by Outlet Establishment Year
* Outlet-wise comparison of all KPIs

## Tools & Features

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* Slicers for interactive filtering
* Conditional Formatting
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Dashboard Design and Data Visualization

## Outcome

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of Blinkit's business performance, helping identify top-performing products, outlet trends, and customer preferences through interactive and visually intuitive reports.
